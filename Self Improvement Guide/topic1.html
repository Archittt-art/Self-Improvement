Understanding Goal Setting

Goal setting is a powerful process for thinking about your ideal future and motivating yourself to turn your vision of this future into reality. Here’s how you can set clear, actionable goals:

  • Make your goals **specific** and **measurable**.
  • Set **realistic** timeframes for achieving them.
  • Regularly review your progress.

Motivation Tips

To stay motivated, focus on your “why” and celebrate small wins along the way.
